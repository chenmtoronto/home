Glossary

3D printing - The process or making a physical object from a digital 3D model.

High resolution - Shows a large amount of details.

Infuse - To combine different substances together to form a solution.

Injection molding - A process of making objects using material that can be injected into the mold.

Efficient - Productive without much waste.

Expensive - Cost a large amount of money.

Manufacture - The process of making of producing something using machines or by hand.

Nesting - When parts are put in different orientations in order to maximize space usage.
